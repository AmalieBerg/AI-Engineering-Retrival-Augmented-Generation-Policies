# Expense Reimbursement Policy

**Document ID:** POL-FIN-001
**Version:** 4.0
**Effective Date:** April 1, 2025
**Owner:** Finance

## 1. Purpose

This policy governs the reimbursement of business-related expenses incurred by employees on behalf of Northwind Technologies. It is intended to ensure consistency, accountability, and compliance with tax regulations.

## 2. General Principles

All expenses must be:

- **Business-related:** Directly tied to Northwind's operations or strategic objectives
- **Reasonable:** Comparable to what a prudent person would spend in similar circumstances
- **Documented:** Supported by itemized receipts and a clear business purpose

Expenses that are personal, lavish, or unsupported will not be reimbursed.

## 3. Submission Process

Expense reports must be submitted via the Expensify system within **30 calendar days** of the expense being incurred. Reports submitted more than 60 days after the expense date will be reimbursed only with VP-level approval and may be treated as taxable income to the employee.

Each report must include:

- An itemized receipt for any expense of **$25 or more**
- A description of the business purpose
- Names of attendees for any meal or entertainment expense
- The associated project code or cost center

## 4. Travel Expenses

### 4.1 Airfare

Economy class is required for all domestic flights. Premium economy is permitted for international flights of more than **6 hours** of flight time. Business class requires VP-level approval, except for flights of more than 10 hours, where business class is permitted by default.

### 4.2 Lodging

Hotel rates should not exceed the following daily limits without justification:

- **Tier 1 cities (NYC, SF, London, Tokyo):** $350/night
- **Tier 2 cities (Boston, Seattle, Berlin):** $275/night
- **All other locations:** $200/night

### 4.3 Meals

Per diem meal allowances are:

- **Breakfast:** $20
- **Lunch:** $30
- **Dinner:** $60

Alternatively, employees may submit itemized receipts up to a daily total of **$110** for meals. Alcohol is reimbursable only as part of an approved client entertainment event and is capped at 2 drinks per person.

### 4.4 Ground Transportation

Ride-share services (Uber, Lyft) and taxis are reimbursable for business travel. Rental cars require manager approval and should be compact or mid-size class unless transporting more than 3 passengers.

Personal vehicle mileage is reimbursed at the current IRS standard rate of **$0.67 per mile** (2025 rate).

## 5. Client Entertainment

Client meals and entertainment expenses must:

- Be pre-approved by a director or above if exceeding **$500 per event**
- Include a clear documented business purpose
- List all attendees by name and company affiliation

Gifts to clients are capped at **$100 per recipient per calendar year** and must comply with the recipient's organization's gift policies.

## 6. Professional Development

Each employee receives an annual professional development budget of **$2,000** for courses, certifications, conferences, and books. Unused budget does not carry over.

Conference attendance exceeding $2,500 in total cost (registration, travel, lodging) requires VP approval.

## 7. Cell Phone and Internet Stipend

Employees in roles requiring frequent off-hours availability (on-call engineers, executives, sales) receive a monthly stipend of:

- **$75/month** for cell phone service
- **$50/month** for home internet (in addition to home office stipend)

## 8. Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following are not reimbursable:

- Traffic violations, parking tickets, or fines
- Personal entertainment (movies, spa, gym membership) unless part of an approved offsite
- First-class airfare without explicit C-suite approval
- Pet care or childcare
- Loss or theft of personal property
- Tips exceeding 20% of the pre-tax amount

## 9. Audit and Compliance

Finance reserves the right to audit any expense report. Employees found to have submitted fraudulent or inflated expenses are subject to disciplinary action up to and including termination. Reimbursement of fraudulent expenses must be repaid in full.

## 10. Questions

Direct expense policy questions to expenses@northwind.example or your Finance business partner.
