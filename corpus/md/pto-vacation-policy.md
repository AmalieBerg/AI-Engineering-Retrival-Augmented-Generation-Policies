# PTO and Vacation Policy

**Document ID:** POL-HR-001
**Version:** 2.4
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**Owner:** People Operations

## 1. Purpose

This policy outlines Northwind Technologies' approach to paid time off (PTO) and vacation. It is designed to ensure employees have adequate time for rest, recovery, and personal matters while maintaining business continuity.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all full-time and part-time employees of Northwind Technologies. Contractors and interns are covered under separate agreements.

## 3. PTO Accrual

Full-time employees accrue PTO based on tenure as follows:

- **0–2 years of service:** 15 days per year (1.25 days per month)
- **3–5 years of service:** 20 days per year (1.67 days per month)
- **6+ years of service:** 25 days per year (2.08 days per month)

Part-time employees accrue PTO on a pro-rata basis according to their scheduled hours.

PTO begins accruing on the employee's first day of employment. New hires may use accrued PTO after completing 90 days of continuous service.

## 4. Carryover and Cap

Employees may carry over up to 5 unused PTO days into the following calendar year. Any PTO above this cap as of December 31 will be forfeited. Northwind does not pay out unused PTO except upon separation, where any accrued and unused PTO will be paid out at the employee's current base salary rate.

## 5. Requesting PTO

PTO requests must be submitted through the Workday system at least **10 business days in advance** for absences of 3 or more consecutive days, and at least **3 business days in advance** for shorter absences. Same-day requests are permitted only for illness or emergencies and must be communicated to the employee's direct manager.

Manager approval is required for all PTO requests. Managers should respond within 2 business days.

## 6. Sick Leave

Sick leave is separate from PTO. Employees receive 10 paid sick days per calendar year, which reset on January 1. Unused sick days do not carry over. A doctor's note is required for sick absences of more than 3 consecutive days.

## 7. Bereavement Leave

Employees are entitled to up to 5 days of paid bereavement leave for the death of an immediate family member (spouse, domestic partner, parent, child, sibling, or in-law). Up to 2 days are provided for extended family members.

## 8. Jury Duty

Northwind provides paid leave for jury duty for up to 10 business days. Employees must provide a copy of the jury summons to People Operations within 5 business days of receipt.

## 9. Blackout Periods

Certain teams may have blackout periods during which PTO requests will not be approved except in emergencies. These include:

- **Engineering:** Final 2 weeks of each quarter (release windows)
- **Finance:** January 1–31 (year-end close) and the first 10 business days of each quarter
- **Sales:** Final 5 business days of each quarter

## 10. Disputes

PTO disputes should be escalated first to the employee's manager, then to People Operations at people-ops@northwind.example. Final appeals may be directed to the VP of People.
