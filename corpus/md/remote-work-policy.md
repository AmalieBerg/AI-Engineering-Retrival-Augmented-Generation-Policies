# Remote Work Policy

**Document ID:** POL-HR-002
**Version:** 3.1
**Effective Date:** March 15, 2025
**Owner:** People Operations

## 1. Overview

Northwind Technologies supports flexible work arrangements that allow eligible employees to perform their duties from a remote location. This policy defines the framework for remote, hybrid, and in-office work arrangements.

## 2. Work Arrangement Types

Northwind recognizes three work arrangement categories:

- **Fully Remote:** Employee works from a remote location 100% of the time and is not assigned to a specific office.
- **Hybrid:** Employee is expected to work from a Northwind office a minimum of **2 days per week**. Specific in-office days are coordinated with the employee's manager.
- **In-Office:** Employee works from a Northwind office 5 days per week.

Default classification for new hires is hybrid, unless the role is explicitly posted as fully remote or in-office.

## 3. Eligibility

To be eligible for fully remote or hybrid arrangements, an employee must:

- Have completed at least **6 months** of continuous employment at Northwind (waived for roles hired as fully remote)
- Hold a role that does not require physical presence (e.g., lab work, on-site IT support)
- Have a current performance rating of "Meets Expectations" or higher
- Maintain manager approval

Roles explicitly excluded from remote eligibility include: facilities staff, lab technicians, on-site security, and certain customer support functions designated by the Customer Experience leadership team.

## 4. Workspace Requirements

Remote employees are responsible for maintaining a workspace that:

- Has reliable internet connectivity with a minimum download speed of **50 Mbps** and upload speed of **10 Mbps**
- Is free from distractions during scheduled work hours
- Provides a professional background for video calls (or uses a Northwind-approved virtual background)
- Meets basic ergonomic standards (adjustable chair, monitor at eye level)

Northwind provides a one-time **$750 home office stipend** to fully remote employees and a **$400 stipend** to hybrid employees, reimbursable upon submission of receipts within the first 90 days of remote eligibility.

## 5. Equipment

Northwind provides the following standard equipment to remote employees:

- Company-issued laptop (MacBook Pro or Dell XPS, employee choice)
- External monitor (up to 27 inches)
- Keyboard and mouse
- Headset for video conferencing

Equipment requests beyond the standard package require manager approval and may be expensed up to **$300** per year through the IT procurement system.

## 6. Working Hours and Availability

Remote employees are expected to be available during their team's **core hours**, defined as 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM in the employee's local time zone, unless otherwise agreed with their manager. Outside of core hours, employees may flex their schedule to accommodate personal needs, provided they complete a standard 40-hour work week.

## 7. Time Zone Coordination

Employees working from time zones outside the United States must coordinate with their manager to establish overlap windows with the team. A minimum of **3 hours of overlap** with the team's primary time zone is required.

## 8. International Remote Work

Working from outside the country of employment record requires advance approval from People Operations and Legal. Short-term work-from-anywhere is permitted for up to **30 calendar days per year** in tax-favorable jurisdictions, subject to approval.

## 9. Security Requirements

All remote work must be conducted on Northwind-issued equipment. Employees must follow the Information Security Policy (POL-SEC-001), including use of the company VPN when accessing internal systems, and must not use public Wi-Fi without VPN protection.

## 10. Termination of Remote Arrangement

Northwind reserves the right to terminate or modify a remote work arrangement with **30 days written notice**, including for business needs, performance concerns, or policy violations. Employees may request a change in their work arrangement at any time by submitting a request to their manager and People Operations.
