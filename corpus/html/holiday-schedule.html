2025 Holiday Schedule

Document ID: POL-HR-004   |   Version: 1.0   |   Effective: January 1, 2025

1. Overview

Northwind Technologies observes a set of company-paid holidays each year. Offices are closed and most employees are not expected to work on these days. This page lists the 2025 schedule by region. Where local laws differ, the local schedule takes precedence.

3. Winter Closure

In addition to the holidays above, Northwind closes all U.S. offices from December 24, 2025 through January 1, 2026. These days are provided as additional paid time off and do not count against the employee's PTO balance.

Employees in customer-facing or on-call roles may be required to provide minimal coverage during the winter closure. Coverage rotations are managed by team leads and are eligible for on-call compensation per the On-Call Policy (POL-HR-007).

6. India Holidays (Bangalore office, 12 paid)

India employees observe 12 paid holidays, including 8 fixed national holidays plus 4 floating holidays chosen by the employee from a list maintained by the India People Operations team. Floating holiday elections must be submitted by January 31 of each year.

7. Floating Holidays

In addition to the fixed schedule, all employees receive 2 floating holidays per calendar year that may be used at the employee's discretion for religious observances, cultural celebrations, or personal events. Floating holidays do not carry over to the next year if unused.

8. Holiday Pay

Non-exempt employees who are required to work on a company holiday receive their regular pay plus a holiday pay premium of 1.5x their base hourly rate. Exempt employees who work on a holiday receive an alternative day off, to be scheduled within 60 days.
