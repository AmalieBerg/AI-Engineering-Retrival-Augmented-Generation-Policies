BUSINESS TRAVEL POLICY
=======================

Document ID: POL-FIN-003
Version: 2.5
Effective Date: April 15, 2025
Owner: Finance & Travel Operations


1. SCOPE
--------
This policy covers all business travel by Northwind employees, including
domestic, international, and offsite events. It supplements (and where
inconsistent, supersedes) the Expense Reimbursement Policy (POL-FIN-001)
for travel-related expenses.


2. APPROVAL REQUIREMENTS
------------------------
All business travel must be pre-approved through the Concur Travel system.
Approval thresholds:

  Domestic travel (same country):   Manager approval
  International travel:              Director approval
  Travel exceeding $5,000 total:     VP approval
  Travel to elevated-risk locations: VP + Security approval (see Section 9)


3. BOOKING REQUIREMENTS
-----------------------
All travel must be booked through Concur Travel using the corporate AmEx
account. Bookings made outside the system will be reimbursable only with
documented justification (e.g., emergency travel, system outage).

Booking lead times:
  - Domestic flights:        Minimum 14 days in advance when possible
  - International flights:   Minimum 21 days in advance when possible

Last-minute bookings (within 7 days) for non-urgent business require
manager justification.


4. PREFERRED VENDORS
--------------------
Northwind has negotiated rates with the following preferred vendors. Use
them whenever practical:

  Airlines:    Delta, United, American (domestic);
               Lufthansa, British Airways, JAL (international)
  Hotels:      Marriott, Hilton, IHG (chains with Northwind corporate rate)
  Car Rental:  Hertz, Enterprise
  Ride-share:  Uber for Business (linked to corporate account)


5. CLASS OF SERVICE
-------------------
Refer to POL-FIN-001 Section 4 for full airfare class rules. Quick summary:

  - Domestic flights:                  Economy only
  - International flights < 6 hours:   Economy only
  - International flights 6-10 hours:  Premium economy permitted
  - International flights > 10 hours:  Business class permitted
  - First class:                       C-suite approval required for any leg


6. EXTENDING TRIPS FOR PERSONAL TIME
-------------------------------------
Employees may extend a business trip for personal time, provided:

  - The business portion is unaffected
  - Northwind pays only the cost of the business portion (the employee
    must demonstrate that no additional cost is incurred)
  - PTO is used for personal-extension days
  - Manager is informed in advance


7. SPOUSE OR FAMILY TRAVEL
--------------------------
Spouses and family members may not travel on the corporate account.
Northwind does not reimburse spouse or family travel expenses, with the
following exceptions:

  - C-suite executives may bring a spouse to designated business events
    (Board meetings, major industry events) with CEO approval
  - Approved relocation travel covered under POL-HR-008


8. TRAVEL INSURANCE
-------------------
All international travel is automatically covered by Northwind's corporate
travel insurance policy, including:

  - Medical emergency coverage up to $500,000
  - Trip interruption coverage
  - Lost baggage coverage
  - Emergency evacuation services

For details and emergency contacts, see the Travel Insurance card included
in the Concur booking confirmation, or contact travel@northwind.example.


9. ELEVATED-RISK LOCATIONS
---------------------------
Travel to locations classified as elevated risk by Northwind's Security
team requires additional approval and may require a pre-departure security
briefing. The current list of elevated-risk locations is maintained at
intranet.northwind.example/security/travel-risk.

Travel to locations on the prohibited list is not permitted under any
circumstances. The prohibited list currently includes countries under
comprehensive U.S. sanctions.


10. DAILY EXPENSE LIMITS
------------------------
Daily limits for travel-related expenses (see POL-FIN-001 for full details):

  Lodging:    $200-$350/night depending on city tier
  Meals:      $110/day (or per diem: $20 breakfast, $30 lunch, $60 dinner)
  Incidentals: $25/day (tips, water, in-room snacks)


11. CANCELLATION AND CHANGE FEES
---------------------------------
Cancellation or change fees are reimbursable when:

  - The change is due to a business need
  - The change is due to a documented illness or emergency
  - The change is due to a flight disruption beyond the employee's control

Cancellation fees for purely personal reasons are not reimbursable.


12. FREQUENT FLYER AND HOTEL POINTS
------------------------------------
Employees may retain frequent flyer miles and hotel loyalty points earned
through business travel for personal use. However, employees must not
choose more expensive options (flights, hotels) solely to accumulate
points. Status matches and benefits earned through business travel are at
the discretion of the loyalty program.


13. POST-TRIP REPORTING
-----------------------
Expense reports for travel must be submitted within 30 days of return
trip date. Reports submitted after 60 days may be denied per POL-FIN-001.


For questions, contact travel@northwind.example or your Finance partner.
